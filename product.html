// About LIPO-C

Get your daily dose of vitamin C, more effectively with Lipo-C!

HALAL حلال Made in Malaysia, Bumiputera product First liposomal vitamin C in Malaysia, established in 2013

Lipo-C Liposomal Vitamin C contains botanical extracts from peach, orange, honey, virgin coconut oil (VCO), medium-chain triglycerides (MCT) oil, turmeric and Stevia enriched with sodium ascorbate and ascorbic acid (vitamin C).

Lipo-C botanical beverage ingredients are coated in fat (liposome), allowing the vitamin C and other nutrients to pass through the high-acid stomach environment and flow directly to the small intestine, where it is then more easily absorbed into the bloodstream. With liposomal delivery system, your body is able to absorb up to 80-90% the amount of vitamin C than what tablets and capsules can offer. This aids in better vitamin C absorbency directly into our body cells; resulting in optimal vitamin C utilization by the body.

Lipo-C contains 30 sachets per box and each sachet has 10ml of botanical beverage enriched with 1000 mg of vitamin C.
